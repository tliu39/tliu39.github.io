Reports

A Survey on Basic Population Genetics Models

This is a survey on several population genetics models, including the Wright-Fisher model, Neutral Moran Model and Biased Moran Model. It specifically focus on the fixation time and extinction time of the original allele and its mutant type. PDF

A Study on Epidemic Spreading Models

This is a study on several epidemic spreading models, including the branching process model, the suspect-infected-recovered model and the Yule process model. The average outbreak size of those models are extensively studied in this writeup. Those models are also applicable in bacterial growth. PDF

Projects

Predicting Housing Prices

Several regression models are proposed to sale price of the houses in a US city. This is based on slightly less than 2000 samples. About 1600 samples are in the training dataset, and the testing dataset contains 350 samples. A testing rmse of around $21000 has been achieved by the final model. The full project is available on the Github.
